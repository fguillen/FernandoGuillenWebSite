Who I am

I'm Fernando Guillen, born in an small city of the north of Spain on 1976. I have been living on a bunch of capitals around three continents.

My academic history is half part technical and half part artistic. But the most of my skills have built in the experience.

I'm a cabinetmaker of the web development and I have a long history on this profession.. still loving it.

What I do

I do high quality ad-hoc web applications development.

What I don't do

Although I love beautiful things, I am used to design my own pet-projects and I consider my self like a creative person I am not a graphic professional designer. I don't do professional web design. Amongst other things because I'm not good on it. I you don't know who ask for the graphic side of your project I can offer you a few very confident contacts.

I don't do multi-purpose CMS integration, if your needs could be implemented using one of the thousand generic software solutions there are on the Internet, a lot of them for free, I'm not the best guy for help you. I am where something exclusive is need.

What I offer is not a commodity, I can offer nothing to you if what you want is something that any programmer can do.

Why hiring me

I'm not competitive, I always will say honestly if I'm the appropriate person to face up your project, or if not, I'll try to offer you possible colleagues that can solve it better than me.

As I don't offer fixed estimations I can afford client decisions changes, and as I am an agile development fan I can face up them as quickly as possible.

I use the most productive web development tools so I can offer same, sometimes more, quality on less time.

Although my speciality is the backside development, I have knowledge, some parts more other less, of every phase and layer of the web development. Functionality analysis, team management, usability decisions, XHTML/CSS, front script technics, servers configuration and maintenance, databases, etc.

I'm on continuos and friendly contact with very high-tech professionals and we like to help one to each other in case of doubt or support needs.

I really concern on the client satisfaction, I never have left a client in the lurch, and, if my memory serves me correctly, I have always carried out the expectations. If someone thinks different but he hasn't told to me, please, do it.

How hiring me

There are a few things I need before I can say if I'm the appropriate person to solve your development needs:

  • PSD or XHTML mock-ups of your web service
  • Desired timing for the first public production version.
  • Budget range available for the development phase.

I also would like to know something from you and the team you are representing, to get to know the people I am going to work with is very important to me.

How I work

I work on weekly milestones, daily activity reports, full and continuos contact with the client.

I work remotely, personal meetings are possible if we decided they will offer something valuable and productive for the project.

Which is my budget

I don't offer fixed budget or fixed timing for the development of a whole project, but on depending the offered mock-ups, the desired timing and the available budget I can offer a approximation.

On depending the dedication the project requires and the timings available my weekly budget is around 750€, plus taxes. If the project requires exclusive dedication or needs something from my leisure and free time the budget will be increased.

Even the simplest web service needs at less 4 or 6 weeks development, without taking into account the possible delay until I finish what I'm doing and I can face up a new project. The great majority takes about 8 weeks until a public beta version, and 4 weeks more fixing and stabilizing the whole functionality.

If personal meetings are necessary travel and accommodation costs will be added to the next invoice.

Payments will be made weekly.
